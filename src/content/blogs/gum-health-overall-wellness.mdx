## Understanding Periodontal Disease

Gum disease is caused by plaque buildup and leads to inflammation, bleeding gums, and eventual tooth loss if untreated.

Oral bacteria can enter the bloodstream and impact other organs.

---

## Oral-Systemic Health Connections

### Heart Disease
Inflammation from gum disease increases cardiovascular risk.

### Diabetes
Blood sugar control worsens gum infections and vice versa.

### Respiratory Problems
Oral bacteria may reach the lungs.

### Pregnancy Risks
Linked to premature birth and low birth weight.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Gum disease impacts whole-body health  
- Heart disease and diabetes are closely linked  
- Oral bacteria can travel through the bloodstream  
- Healthy gums reduce systemic inflammation  
- Regular cleanings protect overall wellness
