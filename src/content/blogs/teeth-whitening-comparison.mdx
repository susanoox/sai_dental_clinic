## Professional Teeth Whitening

Performed by dentists using stronger whitening agents.

### Pros
- Fast results  
- Safer application  
- Even whitening  

### Cons
- Higher cost  
- Temporary sensitivity  

---

## At-Home Whitening Options

- Whitening toothpaste  
- Strips and gels  
- Custom trays  
- Natural remedies (limited proof)  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Professional whitening gives faster results  
- At-home options suit mild stains  
- Dentist supervision ensures safety  
- Sensitivity is usually temporary  
- Best results come from professional advice
