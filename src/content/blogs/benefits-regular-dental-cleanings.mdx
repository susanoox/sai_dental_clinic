## Beyond Brushing and Flossing

Professional cleanings remove tartar that brushing cannot, preventing gum disease and decay.

---

## Benefits of Professional Cleanings

- Prevents gum disease  
- Detects problems early  
- Removes stains  
- Reduces bad breath  
- Protects long-term oral health  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Brushing alone is not enough  
- Tartar causes gum disease  
- Cleanings improve smile appearance  
- Early detection saves teeth  
- Preventive care lowers treatment cost
