## What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots.

They provide stability, durability, and prevent bone loss.

---

## Types of Dental Implants

- Endosteal implants  
- Subperiosteal implants  
- All-on-4 / All-on-6  
- Mini implants  
- Zygomatic implants  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Implants mimic natural teeth  
- Preserve jawbone health  
- Multiple implant options exist  
- Choice depends on bone density  
- Long-lasting tooth replacement solution
