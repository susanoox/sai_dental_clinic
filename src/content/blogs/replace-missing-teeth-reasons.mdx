## The Consequences of Missing Teeth

Missing teeth cause nearby teeth to shift, leading to misalignment, bite issues, and difficulty cleaning. Over time, jawbone loss occurs due to lack of stimulation, altering facial structure.

Chewing inefficiency can also affect digestion and nutrition.

---

## Benefits of Timely Tooth Replacement

- Prevents bone loss  
- Maintains facial appearance  
- Improves chewing and speech  
- Stops neighboring teeth from shifting  
- Enhances confidence  

---

## Key Takeaways

- Missing teeth affect more than appearance  
- Bone loss begins soon after tooth loss  
- Early replacement preserves oral health  
- Implants and bridges restore function  
- Timely action prevents complex treatments
